Genius

I turn my thoughts to my own situation. What justifies existence these days? A source of income. What justifies a source of income? School, endless amounts of it, at incredible cost. Or genius.

The system does not neglect hidden genius. It has a story for that. The bright and shining ones we almost lost, because of our stubborn system. I have played to this tune for as long as I can remember, the part of the hidden gem. Hidden gems do not magically make friends with professors, or anyone who can help them through school. Hidden gems mostly assume that this is a contest, a gauntlet, not a place of learning.

That it is all one test, to measure general worth, or g for short. They want to get the highest g score with the least effort, because this makes everyone impressed. And the way I do this is to mollify the real world, while I play in the fictional. Give them their shiny scores and high numbers, and then go back to what really matters, other worlds.

At one point, I wondered what it would be like to play in reality.

So I tried it. I put myself into the hub world, the level select for all other stories. I wondered what it meant, that everyone was so impressed with the numbers. Numbers like dollars per month or intelligence quotient or RAADS-R or any number of evaluation tools. I wondered what it was, that we had been evaluating.

I think I found the answer. Brains are for communication. This is why they are made. They are cellular internet service. The g everyone wanted was a proxy for the throughput of the network, a combination of space constraints, time constraints, and memorized computation.

I think an additonal variable might be focus constraints, but this is the most complicated of them, because it is necessarily about the hyperobject. Yes, really. If I am focused on something, it is the work of the hyperobject.

Large language models have a thing called attention which allows them to make sense of patterns in long scrolling text. I have imagined it is something like that hyper-glossary I spent yesterday dreaming of, with the infinitely long scroll, the one that finds even the patterns we deliberately ignore.

Words are highlighted when they appear many times. Their multiple instances combine into a shared identity. This identity is annotated. Like a definition. I imagine that the models use fuzzy definitions, gathered from many instances of shared data.

A man says,

I am taken aback when women ask me if I want to go down tracks A, B, C, D, E. Men are simple. We only go down one track. And not even all the way.

I think of Annelang, and its operator &, which is one of the two not seen in other languages.

The function must plan for either case, with or. The dual would be to first write out the plans, then select one later. This gives one A, B, C, D, E all as possibilities.

I call this an alternation type. It is something like exactly one lookup of a table. It is something like selecting one option and getting the next step in response.

I am quite proud of it, because within this type, all things emerge. I wonder if an arrow can be made an exponentiation. Perhaps the other kind: possibility, logarithm, the dual of the familiar.

I have sometimes called it log, after the inverse of exp.

The trouble with exponentiation is, savefiles are weird. If you have a savefile of the procedure to extract something from a savefile, that is itself a savefile of it. This is called Löb's Theorem, and it is true in arithmetic. It is also true in network programming. My objects often had second constructers, taking in object readers. Untyped. Perhaps one could type them.

Point being, objects could be created from descriptions of objects, in plain binary.
