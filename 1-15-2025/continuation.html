Just because something is not of type A, does not mean it is of type B. Just because something is not of type A, does not mean it is of any type at all.

Just because something is not true, does not mean it is false. Just because something is not false, does not mean it is true. The answer could be non-binary.

The analogous form to being nonbinary, over all types in a type universe, is continuation.
