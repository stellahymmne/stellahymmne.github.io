Vow

Choice
Two roads diverged... player picks which road, others are all taboo. Only one way out, but it's your call. Left or right? Looks like free will, but is actually the Player's memory-pattern & choice fn @ System. Thus pathwise & often terrible. Not unrelated to Fate.
Fate
An arrow pointing to the future. Everyone who gets there has one, but they don't know beforehand. Heads or tails? Looks like random chance, is actually the System's memory-pattern & random fn @ Players. Thus pathwise & often terrible. Not unrelated to Choice.
