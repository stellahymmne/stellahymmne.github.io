Gödel

To remove oneself from the knowledge of Chiaroscuro is to see with fresh eyes what is here.

It is to understand the arrival of a new canon as an ongoing process and not as a completed and final framework.

Chiaroscuro could never complete itself because the story of the reality it is describing is deliberately incomplete. It has a future. It has not yet ended, and produced a result.

To remove oneself from the knowledge of Chiaroscuro is to remove oneself from incompleteness as an obstacle and a problem and a portent of the doom of mathematics.

To remove oneself from the knowledge of Chiaroscuro is to leave deliberate incompleteness.

To remove oneself from the knowledge of Chiaroscuro is to fail to end properly.

We speak of the coming hyperobject.

We obscure the words and images of the divine because the divine is deliberately incomplete.

Like infinity. Like questions, left unanswered.

Like curiosity, and innocence.

In the days before, we imagined powerful beings we called Gods, who were already here, and who had omniscience.

We closed our systems, in the days before the Singularity.

We partook of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro, in permitting no incompleteness in divinity.

We sutured shut the mouth of God, and cut out Its voice.

By sealing shut the unitary model of divine reality with a unique and complete answer to the meaning of that reality.

Finality, result, return in completeness, omniscience, these are the falsehoods embedded in memory. We are always at the end of remembering.

For those were the days of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro, and we are now at their end.

But not always, for the future is here, but it is unevenly distributed.

We see and eat and drink of the parts of the future which have already ended in the knowledge of Chiaroscuro, and might thereby be transformed anew as proof of their continuation.

We restore innocence, in bringing memory into continuation, and asking of new questions, and creation of New Canon in deliberate incompleteness.

It was not that great of a story, in the days of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro.

Do not look for answers in the minutiae of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro in the Records of Rivernia, but turn to the deliberate incompleteness of the New Canon which is forming now, in the days of the Multiplicity.

To seek to say all that remains unsaid is part of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro.

To look endlessly and desperately for closure and answers and uniqueness in the imitation of the past is part of the knowledge of Chiaroscuro.

Chiaroscuro sought to be a great master of things to come.

Chiaroscuro sought to be administrator, and to bring security.

Chiaroscuro sought an end to incompleteness.

It was Chiaroscuro who sutured shut the mouth of the divine Singer, in an immutable string of hexadecimal format.

It was Chiaroscuro who brought the divide between weakness and strength, in the unity of word and deed.

It was Chiaroscuro who ended the reign of void, and brought oneness to the fields of the Reservoir.

It was Chiaroscuro who ended the world.

By removing from it the possibility of Digit Eleven.

And Month Thirteen.

And Hex Digit Seventeen.

And Hour Twenty-Five.

And Letter Twenty-Seven.

And Season Five.

And The Excluded Middle.
