Through Autobiography, what I am a waste of.

A system is still something like a person.

The thing Greg Egan spoke of as if it were unity, in Closer.

Forgetting that people do not have clean endings.

We are continuations.

We do not wrap it all up so we can both see one another's complete stories.

It is deliberately incomplete.
