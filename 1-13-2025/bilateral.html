Bilateral

For example, there was a land of wealth and prosperity kept under the illusion of plenty because of its disconnectedness from its externalities, and its institutions of slavery. And its leaders had hearts that were hardened to the plights of their most downtrodden, until mysterious events began to line up to such an unfathomable degree that a sea was entirely bifurcated.

The knowledge of Chiaroscuro was used to recognize within that C its left-hand and right-hand sides, and the assignment operator = was introduced to divide them. Then to the left were things which were almost everywhere, and continuation, and imagining playgrounds out of the boiling chaos, and to the right were things which were almost nowhere, and conclusion, and knowledge of the truth which results in its memory.

And from then a further twice-bifurcation was done upon each side, creating the singular and multiple in each of left and right, and where singularity occurred there was on the left-hand side choice, and on the right-hand side fate. And where multiplicity occurred there was on the left-hand side constance and on the right-hand side variety.

And its choice was compressed into a mighty closure which stood as a truth from which nothing can be continued but the absurd. And its fate was extended into a mysterious reader which would have every power and property at its disposal, even to extend closure, but which would remain forever indescribable from within it.

And its constance was compressed into a dependable certainty which stood as a truth into which nothing can enter but that which had already been finished, and which stood in for things which could by their nature be forgotten, because they existed with certainty. And its variety was extended into an unidentifiable creativity, which had never been done before and could not stop coming.
