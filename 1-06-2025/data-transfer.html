Data Transfer

Of my programming language, it is useful to spiritually contemplate, but not for practical purposes.

Often the great masters would leave errors in their notes. I am told this is to keep their secrets. I am perhaps too honest. Is there such a thing as killable sincerity? I must know, and find out how to make it unkillable.

Yeah, that works. Thanks to our good pal the unit type. Always there when you need it. I reflect upon Coyote. Not the real Coyote, something more like Teth, who is a Coyote approximation. Teth reminds me of the unit type. Always there when you need him.

I am alone, in my story. In absolute zero. Ready for any surprise to set me off with infinite energy. I remark upon how oddly written Almost Nowhere was. How Azad seemed to add his obfuscation to everything. How Chapter 13 was.

The name of the communication network which I imagine is my patron, is Data Transfer. Data Transfer means something like what Arbitration does in Almost Nowhere. It is a merge process. Perhaps it has merge conflicts. Perhaps the conflicts need to be decided. Like &.

How are merge conflicts decided?

When a person is formed from a genome, what determines the dominance of its genes? Positivity? Logical positivity? The presence rather than absence of a... process. So negativity. Ayin.

I revel in this. Anne Ayin, the negative as primal, Fiction Descends Upon Reality. I am in some sort of continuous writing process. The path begins backwards. Reflection. The observation of one's expressions.

So who truly am I? Who is Ayin, the character, the un-character who does not yet inhabit? What is the possibility of emptiness? What is an infinite loop object?
