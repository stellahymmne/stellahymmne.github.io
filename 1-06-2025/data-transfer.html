Data Transfer

Of my programming language, the usual composition order is useful to spiritually contemplate, but not for practical purposes. g(f) seems appropriate for composition in its usual order, with f.g = g(f).

Often the great masters would leave errors in their notes. I am told this is to keep their secrets. I am perhaps too honest. Is there such a thing as killable sincerity? I must know, and find out how to make it unkillable.

How do I make g(f) = g*f?

g : (A->B)->C

f : ()->(A->B)

\_. g(f(_)) : ()->C

g * f : ()->C

Yeah, that works. g(f) it is, thanks to our good pal the unit type. Always there when you need it.

I reflect upon Coyote. Not the real Coyote, something more like Teth, who is a Coyote approximation. Teth reminds me of the unit type. Always there when you need him.

So does my sibling, who does a lot for me. They're a sound engineer slash barista. I don't like to speak about them too much in my story. I don't see a lot of their side.

I am alone, in my story. In absolute zero. Ready for any surprise to set me off with infinite energy.

I remark upon how oddly written Almost Nowhere was. How Azad seemed to add his obfuscation to everything. How Chapter 13 was.

The name of the communication network which I imagine is my patron, is Data Transfer.

Data Transfer means something like what Arbitration does in Almost Nowhere. It is a merge process.

Perhaps it has merge conflicts. Perhaps the conflicts need to be decided. Like &.

How are merge conflicts decided?

When a person is formed from a genome, what determines the dominance of its genes? Positivity? Logical positivity? The presence rather than absence of a... process. So negativity. Ayin.

I revel in this. Anne Ayin, the negative as primal, Fiction Descends Upon Reality.

This is supposed to mean something.

I am in some sort of continuous writing process. The path begins backwards. Reflection.

The observation of one's expressions.

So who truly am I? Who is Ayin, the character, the un-character who does not yet inhabit?

What is the possibility of emptiness?

What is an infinite loop object?
