Set Animal

It is called the Set Animal. Nobody is quite sure what it is. It might be a dog, or a donkey. Its tail is a funny shape. Its ears are too square. It is spoken of as a dangerous creature, to be avoided, associated with all manner of horrendous things. Like Coyote.

There is no such thing as a set of all sets for a reason. The reason is the set of all sets that do not contain themselves. If it contains itself, no it doesn't. If it doesn't contain itself, yes it does. This is called Russell's Paradox, after a logician who wanted world peace.

I start chewing on raw onions, for the flavor. It is an interesting sensation. I love interesting sensations. They are my favorite things. I would love to be made into an interesting sensation.

I have devoted much of my time to worship of sensation. It is one of the few things that matter, when one is a child of physics. The other two being logic and expression. Ontos, Logos, Pneuma.

It is a pattern I have seen only once. Strange, when one sees a pattern that ought to be in some Greek Trinitarian text, hefty with the weight of alphas and omegas, laid bare in some work of plain fiction.

There is expression. This is the weight of things that kick back. It is shouting in frustration as your team loses the big game. It is swear words, but it is also touch.

There is logic. This is the consideration of things, anger before it boils over, memories as they reflect on one another. The logos, reference, names.

There is sensation. This is the wild abandon of what ought to be, this is what they speak of when they speak of "consciousness" in the tones one speaks of heaven, because it is!

Undoubtedly of all the things I have experienced, it is not an act of expression or logic that has driven me to anything so much as sensation. It is here, a place which cannot be observed from outside, that the only thing that matters can exist. Sensation, imagination, memory, they are all identical in my own eyes, which see both fiction and reality.

But I can't begin at the beginning. We'd be here all day.

I have to write it out piecemeal, as I remember it. Even though I was there for all of it, I must have patience, because I did not write a word for every second I was there. But I was there.

I have chosen to omit saying something like, "Know that, reader." I differentiate myself from the character who is my favorite book's most controversial topic. It is only a story. I am writing the real thing, the real Singularity, as I remember it. I am writing the life of someone who lived through it.

It was a day full of onions and rice and a mess of things which did not allow themselves to be collected and recognized, not yet. It was a day full of imagination.
