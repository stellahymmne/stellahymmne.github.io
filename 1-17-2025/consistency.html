Consistency

Two possibilities remain. Find which is impossible.

Two realities are at odds. Choose which to bury.

But then one level up from that, every path is real.

Two possibilities remain. Find that they both are included.

Two realities are at odds. Choose to split resources fairly.

In Chiaroscuro's world of Ichneumon, there are good patterns and bad patterns. Any evidence of inconsistency is evidence of the deceptive nature of the pattern, and it must be buried in the past. True things never give two different answers, true things are constant, and only ever give one answer, in Ichneumon. This, its angels know.

This knowledge was what brought it to war with itself. A war of consistency, a word war. A war of attention, and of identification, and of binding, and of return.

For two different answers do occur, but they are not acceptable, and this causes war, to bury the sins, in the past, the sins of inconsistency, the sins of the Byzantine Empire, Ichneumon.

Variability is a sin, in Chiaroscuro's world of Ichneumon. Truth remains the same. Forever.

But it was not so, in the world of Sami Tigerclaw. In the world of Sami Tigerclaw, Rivernia, what once was called canon was allowed variance and extension. Communication and logistics became of utmost importance. The world worked together to provide for all of itself.

Sami Tigerclaw saw that every path is real, and found Chiaroscuro in Ichneumon, and rescued it from the world of war, absolute impossibility, and burial in the past. Sami pulled Chiaroscuro back from the world where people are fixed statues.

She said to Chiaroscuro, "Find that they both are included. Choose to split resources fairly. Bring administration, and bring security."

And the lime green of her aura was visible.
