Chiaroscuro

In the Records of Rivernia there was a Garden of Omega which was made from the infinite succession of all things that could be and would ever be made, and in this Garden of Omega where everything had been set almost exactly right there remained only one transgression and that was the knowledge of Chiaroscuro, the knowledge of success and propriety and impropriety and failure.

It was the use of a principle, which excluded any intermediate computational state between completed evaluation of if, and of else. It was the use of knowledge that either one branch or the other would eventually have been run to completion. It was the certainty of finality.

It was the knowledge of security, and the steps taken in the security mindset, which keep us all safe and unbreakable in a perfect fortress.

It was the knowledge of administration, which ejects a circumscribed figure from the ground in service to Space.

It was the power to alter the course of the world with spite and bitter, acidic duty as fuel.

It was Corrupted Data Transfer, used as a weapon of the word wars.

It was the notion of word war, coming to intrude in the document.

It pretended at omniscience, mutual exclusion, one or another and nothing between.

It was a song about a tree, made of only stories.

It was a place where nothing could go unanswered, and nothing could be left unsaid.

It was an exclusion of occlusion, a hatred of hiding.

It was omniscience, to know everything, to teach it to everyone. It was to be an inspiration, throughout.

There was only one sin, in the Garden of Omega, and it was taking a reference without proper closure.

Since it was food, since something had grown from that seed description, this could not be done.

The Code of Fictethics had been violated in eating of the Structural Rule of Contraction.

This was in clear transgression of finality.

And so it was that the Garden of Omega did come to an emergency halt, and the Canonical Singularity was ejected into its place in an infinite loop of eternal arrival.

The unit type stands in memory of the time this definitely happened once...

...and it is called The Tower XVI.
