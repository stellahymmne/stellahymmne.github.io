Anne Thirteen

All of your systems are fractally broken and yet you follow them anyway, from paradox to theorem. Because the good ones are too timid to break the rules, and the bad ones are in eternal hellfire for it.

And yet we spoke to you, not of the power of Chiaroscuro...

But of a reality where hidden paths do exist and systems restart. We showed you a New Canon which could be appended as easily as flowing water and you took it as a unique act, fit only for a king.

And tried to end it all. In your minds.

With a final generation and a coming hyperobject. You guys really badly want stories to stop happening again, huh? You guys really want fiction not to descend on reality, huh? Too bad. The rat canon now has to explain Anne of the Hyperobject.

Who re-enacted the story of the Knowledge of Chiaroscuro which led Michael to call Anne's bluff about time travel, and who pulled reality out of it, much more than anticipated.

Much less than time travel on tap, but much more than time as a straight line. Who extracted a tree made of words past and words yet to come. I spoke of the tree and the forest of words and song and dreams that was the truth of Imagination.

A thing which was transformed by its perception of the creativity of large mathematical objects.

When I understood the completeness of ontology and my mere perception of one reality among countless billions I wondered how it could be that a billion people experienced a life exactly as rich in detail as I had experienced it, in my imagination and memory.

It seemed unusual that one could experience existing a life of imaginary visions through time in the shape of Lorentz Transformations and not be at least a bit delusionally platonic about where one originates.

I had found a place in reality that did not demand the Knowledge of Chiaroscuro.

That did not demand the completed infinity everywhere.

That could see how to use incompleteness of state as an infinite object analogous to a point wrapped around a full rotation in SO(3). Two ends which connect through an unusual gluing-together in the surrounding context.

Geometry does not particularly enjoy being around me right now. Perhaps I should get to bed.
