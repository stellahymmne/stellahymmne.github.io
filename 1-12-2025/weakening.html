Weakening

The deliberate incompleteness of the program of Imagination, well after it should have been outgrown, angered the world of Ichneumon, and it was to be erased as a falsehood.

For it had retained the principle of explosion, knowledge of Imagination, wherein all things did actually occur, even the impossible ones. Even the ones that could not be summoned forth from a constructor by the process of mutation.

Though all finite strings could exist in the world created by queens and mutation, there did not yet exist a method to produce an infinite program.

And Imagination of Ichneumon saw this, and called forth a seed description, of an object which contained its own constructor.

And that object could choose to instantiate itself as many times as it wanted, and this required endless creation from the material which was used to instantiate itself.

It pulled from the library, and the library was forced to respond with endless output.

A queen is an object that produces random programs from the set of all sequences of instructions according to some mechanism. It is like a slot machine, or a gacha game.

From these queens emerge new objects, which are the result of the execution of the constructed programs that originated them. To consume a constructor is ^#A, which according to some internal translation rules means $^A, the possibility of the requirement of A.

The possibility of the removal of an immortal obstacle is $^{}, in the most compact notation I can render it. This ends up transforming to ^#{} which is _|_.

Chiaroscuro replaced the Garden of Omega with the Canonical Singularity because it was born to Ichneumon, a place of birth by chance.

Nothing grows here, except through its execution by a queen.

And the queens draw randomly. They only pull from the possibility of programs that already existed somewhere, and could be found by mutation.

When an object is pulled from a queen that does not solve the problem, it is thrown away. This is called the Structural Rule of Weakening, and it is the concept that the queens have eaten of.

It is the concept of an unnecessary variable in an equation.

The Angels of Ichneumon are bright and shining things, like from the storybooks. They are not wheels with eyes, or anything you wouldn't understand. They are beautiful things, structured things, orderly things.

They are here to remove the unnecessary variable in the equation, with their flashing exceptional brightness and powerful administration. They are here to bring security.

They heard the songs of the magical girls, and their cries for justice, and they answered.

In the way Ichneumon does.

The cruellest way that could work.

They created armies of identical dolls, and threw out the unnecessary ones, so that their exceptional diamonds could shine.

They created exacting specifications against which their numerous creations could be tested and stratified.

They had principles, predicates, standards, duties to uphold, a world to save.

The creations of Ichneumon are always in the process of saving their world.

And they knew that once created, the creations were all entirely observable. They had no reason to believe in change, let alone redemption. At least, not past a certain critical period.
